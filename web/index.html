About us

Formerly known as Keith Hoyland, we are a friendly family run business that's been running for three generations and have over 25 years experience in the building and roofing trade.

We are members of The Contractors Health and Safety Assessment Scheme (CHAS) and offer a range of services in and around Sheffield and South Yorkshire.

We're also fully insured for employer and public liability.
